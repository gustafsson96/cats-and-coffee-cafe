About Us

In many ways, we are just like any other café. In the mood for an energizing cup of coffee? Or why not a comforting cup of tea? A refreshing lemonade, perhaps? We got you covered. We also have several different lunch options and hommeade pastries on our menu.


So, what makes us different? Well, while you are enjoying your coffee or lunch, you are doing so surrounded by our four legged friends. All our cats are rescues and our mission is to help them find their forever homes. Under the section "Our Cats" you can see the cats avaliable at the moment, and if you are interested in adopting we highly encourage you to contact us through the adoption form further down this page. You can also pay us a visit and we will tell you everything you need to know!
