About Us

  • In many ways, we are just like any other café. In the mood for an energizing cup of coffee? A comforting cup of tea? A refreshing lemonade, perhaps? We got you covered. You can also find several different lunch options and hommeade pastries. Visit us to take a look at our menu!


  • So, what makes us different? Well, while you are enjoying your coffee or delicious lunch, you are doing so surrounded by our four legged friends. All our cats are rescues and our mission is to help them find their forever homes. Under the section "Our Cats" you can see the cats avaliable at the moment, and if you are interested in adopting we highly encourage you to contact us through the adoption form further down this page. You can also pay us a visit and we will tell you everything you need to know.


  • Got a question? Send us an email, call, come inside, whatever you prefer! You can find our contact information and social media links at the bottom of this page.
